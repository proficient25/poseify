## Tone & Voice Guidelines

**Professional Yet Approachable**
- Use confident language
- Emphasize partnership and support
- Be aspirational without being pretentious

**Action-Oriented**
- Use active voice
- Clear, direct calls-to-action
- Benefit-focused descriptions

**Industry-Specific**
- Use modeling/marketing terminology appropriately
- Reference relevant services and concepts
- Show expertise through language

**Trustworthy & Credible**
- Highlight experience and relationships
- Include testimonials
- Provide specific service details

---

## Writing Principles Applied

1. **Clarity** - Clear, concise sentences
2. **Benefit-Focused** - Emphasize what clients and models gain
3. **Social Proof** - Testimonials and success metrics
4. **Action** - Strong calls-to-action throughout
5. **Professionalism** - Industry-appropriate language
6. **Authenticity** - Real capabilities and services
7. **Personality** - Confident, passionate voice
8. **Accessibility** - Easy to understand, not overly technical

---

## Usage Tips

✓ When updating content, maintain this tone and voice
✓ Keep messaging client-focused (what's in it for them)
✓ Always include clear CTAs at end of major sections
✓ Use specific examples rather than generic statements
✓ Highlight unique selling points and differentiators
✓ Emphasize professionalism and industry expertise
✓ Include social proof when available
